About us.

Our Story

In 2011, two creatives, Ariho Kamara and Jeremy Byemanzi, had a dream to create the kind of agency that they had always wanted to work for; an agency that gave them a chance to work on compelling projects, with remarkable clients while maintaining a good work/life balance. On July 19th, 2011, Nomad was born! - a brand strategy and digital design agency that lives up to that dream.
In 2012, they were joined by Julius Kwame and the Nomad Rwanda chapter was born.

Nomad is currently located in Kigali, Rwanda Kampala, Uganda and Accra, Ghana.
We’re a value-led african creative agency that creates concepts to empower brands that positively impact individuals, communities and nations. We’re a tight, passionate team driven by the desire to deliver forward thinking ideas to our clients. Together, we create brand identities, design and build websites and deliver marketing campaigns that make an impact.
